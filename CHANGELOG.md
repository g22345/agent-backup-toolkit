## [0.1.0-alpha.1] - 2026-07-17

### Added

- Strict schema-versioned YAML configuration.
- File, bounded-directory, and SQLite collectors.
- Secret detection with redacted findings.
- Deterministic manifests and archives with mandatory `age` encryption.
- Local, private GitHub Releases, and S3-compatible destinations.
- Complete encrypted-artifact and final-receipt read-back verification.
- Preview-first restore and verified encrypted rollback before overwrite.
- Synthetic tests, documentation, and public-tree audit tooling.
